RRC SCR Apprentice Recruitment 2026

1. Introduction

Railway Recruitment Cell (RRC), South Central Railway (SCR) ne Apprentice posts ke liye official notification release kiya hai. Is recruitment ke through 2801 posts bhari jayengi. Jo candidates railway me apprentice banna chahte hain, wo online apply kar sakte hain. Application form 12 March 2026 se start ho chuka hai aur apply karne ki last date 11 April 2026 hai. Is recruitment ke liye minimum age 15 years aur maximum age 24 years rakhi gayi hai (as on 11 April 2026). Reserved category candidates ko government rules ke according age relaxation milega. Candidates ko 10th pass hona chahiye aur related trade me ITI certificate hona zaroori hai. Selection 10th marks ki merit list, document verification aur medical exam ke basis par hoga.
Note: Railway jobs stable government jobs mani jati hain.
