Abhijit Sen Rural Internship (ASRI)

The National Foundation for India Abhijit Sen Rural Internship 2026 is a 50-day field research internship for undergraduate and postgraduate students in India. The program offers students an opportunity to visit rural areas, conduct surveys, interact with communities, and research socio-economic issues. Selected interns will receive mentorship, fieldwork experience, travel and accommodation support, and a ₹20,000 stipend after completing the research report. This internship helps students understand rural development challenges and gain practical research skills.

2. About Internship

The Abhijit Sen Rural Internship 2026 is a 50-day field research internship for undergraduate and postgraduate students in India. The program offers students an opportunity to visit rural areas, conduct surveys, interact with communities, and research socio-economic issues. Selected interns will receive mentorship, fieldwork experience, travel and accommodation support, and a ₹20,000 stipend after completing the research report. This internship helps students understand rural development challenges and gain practical research skills.

3. Internship Highlights

  • Internship Name: Abhijit Sen Rural Internship 2026
  • Organization: National Foundation for India
  • Internship Type: Research & Field Internship
  • Field Work: 30 Days Rural Field Research
  • Benefits: Travel, Food and Accommodation Covered

4. Eligibility Criteria

Applicants must be students from a recognized Indian university or college.

Undergraduate (UG) and Postgraduate (PG) students are eligible to apply.

Students from any stream or academic discipline can apply.

Candidates must have an interest in rural development, social issues, and field research.

Women students and students from Dalit, Adivasi, Tribal, and minority communities are encouraged to apply.

5. Required Skills

  • Basic research and analytical skills
  • Good communication and interaction skills
  • Ability to conduct surveys and interviews
  • Interest in rural development and social issues
  • Basic report writing and documentation skills
  • Ability to work with communities and grassroots organisations
  • Teamwork and problem-solving skills

6. Educational Qualification

Candidates must be pursuing Undergraduate (UG) or Postgraduate (PG) courses. Students from any recognized Indian university or college are eligible. Candidates from any stream or academic background can apply.

7. Internship Duration

The internship is a 50-day program.

8. Internship Stipend

Selected interns will receive a stipend of ₹20,000 after completing the research report.

9. Internship Location

The internship will be conducted in various rural areas across India, including states like Chhattisgarh, Jharkhand, Madhya Pradesh, Odisha, Gujarat, Rajasthan, and Maharashtra.
